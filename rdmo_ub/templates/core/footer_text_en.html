About RDMO

The Research Data Management Organiser @ Goethe University supports the systematic planning, organisation and implementation of data and software management in the course of a research project.

Contact

For information about RDMO@GU, please contact our support team at Frankfurt University Library.

Generic training material such as instructions, documentation, tutorials and more on RDMO can be found at the RDMO community.
