Research data management organiser @ Goethe University

Version {% version %}

With the RDMO@GU (Research Data Management Organiser) service, researchers and their cooperation partners at Goethe University can plan and implement the research data management of their projects in a structured manner. It enables the recording of all relevant planning information in data management plans and the administration of all data management tasks throughout the entire data life cycle.

For more information about RDMO@GU, please contact our RDMO support team at the Goethe University Library.
